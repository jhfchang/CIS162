Elk Family

Yellowstone National Park

I recently had the chance to take a road trip up to Yellowstone National Park for the first time in late May. The weather was mostly pleasant during the day, except for the freezing temperature on our first night—the area had just had some snow the day before. I was really amazed by the variety of landscapes in one park: the forests, the canyons, the geysers, and more. The park also did not disappoint when it came to wildlife sightings. The picture on the left is a close encounter we had with an elk family—they were so friendly that a park ranger had to make some loud noises to deter them. A group of bison walking on the road also stopped traffic for 40 minutes! We were also lucky enough to see a wolf, and a bear from a distance. It was an amazing experience, and it reminded me how refreshing it is to be embraced by nature. I can't wait to get on the road again.
